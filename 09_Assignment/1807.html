Goa University
Goa University was established in 1985 by an Act of the State Legislature, incorporating the then existing Centre for Post Graduate Instruction and Research of the University of Bombay, with 13 teaching departments and 17 affiliated colleges. From a modest beginning in 1985, the University has, over the past twenty-five years, established itself and expanded across 12 faculties, with 24 post graduate departments and three Centres on its campus, with 48 affiliating professional and non-professional colleges.
